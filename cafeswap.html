User Research

Survey (N = 30) shared via social media and personal networks

  • 36.7% visit cafes multiple times a week.
  • Clear market for the app.
  • 69% of respondents discover new drinks from social media platforms such as TikTok and Instagram.
  • Many users discover new drinks from word of mouth, whether thats from friends or in-store suggestions.

    • Takeaway: Providng a solid platform to discover new drinks would consildate the amount of sources users have to go through to find recommendations.

  • A large amount of respondents would be open to the idea of trying a strangers drink .
  • Even the respondents who didn't choose "Absolutely, sounds fun" seem to be open to the idea if they could see reviews/ratings.

    • Takeaway: The "Swap" part of the app can be created as long as their is also a review and ratings section.
